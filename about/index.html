iOS coding is a blog about developing iOS apps. The blog contains posts about my open source projects as well as my thoughts about various things related to iOS and Apple.

On the weekend I work on my own stuff, like open source components I find useful or this blog. When there is time left, I am running my own little one-man business with a couple of apps on the iTunes App Store.
